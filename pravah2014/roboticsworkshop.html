Robotics Club


A Workshop on Autonomous Robotics

Introduction: Robotics is nothing but a part of technology that is, it comprises of mechanical design, electrical and electronics hardware & components and software. Through these workshops you will come to know about the practical use of almost every components used in robotics and also the working of various sensors, collision avoider robots, line follower robots.

Objective: To provide a better project based training in the field of Robotics and to explore new technologies of robotics among the students.

Duration: Workshop will be accomplished in two days (5-6 hours/day).

Fee structure

Each participating studentwill have to pay an amount of Rs. 700/- only. A minimum of 4 members are required in a team.

Benefits of workshop

  • Projects which you can build: Various sensors, Collision avoider robots, Wall follower robots, Path finder robot, Edge avoider robot, Obstacle detector, Equipments controller with PC.
  • After this work shop you will become familiar with all aspects and concepts of robotics.
  • You will be capable to participate in any national and international level robotics competitions.
