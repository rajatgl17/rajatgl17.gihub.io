ROBOMARATHON


Structure

THE PARTICIPANT SHOULD BACK BUILD A MANUALLY CONTROLLED BOT THAT CAN RACE ON A TRACK OF TERRAINS LIKES SAND, SPEED BREAKERS,SPACE ELEVATIONS ETC. THE AIM OF THE EVENT IS TO TEST THE ROBUSTNESS OF THE VEHICLE AND ITS COMPETENCE WITH THE OPPONENT ON THE SAME TRACK.

Rules and Regulation

  • THE BOT DIMENSIONS SHOULD NOT EXCEED 35cm*35cm*7cm.
  • A TEAM WILL CONSIST OF MAXIMUM OF 3 MEMBERS.
  • THE VOLTAGE SUPPLY MUST NOT EXCEED 12V (DC) ANYWHERE B/W THE CIRCUIT.
  • ALL THE BOTS MUST BE DEPENDENT ON ELECTRIC MOTORS ONLY. NO USE OF OTHER POWER TRANSMITTING DEVICES ARE ALLOWED.
  • THE BOT SHOULD ALWAYS REMAIN INSIDE THE TRACK.
  • ANY BOT REMAINING STATIONARY FOR MORE THAN 25 SECONDS WILL BE DISQUALIFIED.
  • ANY BOT DEEMED TO CAUSE HARM TO THE ENVIRONMENT AND ANY OTHER SURROUNDINGS WILL BE DISQUALIFIED.
  • THE EVENT ORGANIZERS HAVE THE RIGHT TO CHANGE THE RULES AND REGULATIONS ANYTIME/WHENEVER NEEDED WITHOUT PRIOR notice.
  • NOTE: ALL THE CANDIDATES ARE REQUESTED TO CARRY THEIR COLLEGE ID CARD ALONG WITH THEM.
