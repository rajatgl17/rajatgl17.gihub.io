Rules and Regulation

  • This is a HINDI rock band competition, open to any college band from India.

  • A group will be required to perform at least one original composition within 25 minutes. (Amps on to amps off inclusive of sound check)

  • No pre-recorded and programmed music is not allowed in any condition.

  • BANDS WILL BE DISQUALIFIED ON THE SPOT FOR MISCONDUCT, OBSCENITY OR FOUL LANGUAGE, AND WILL BE BANNED FROM PERFROMING AT ALL SUSEQUENT EDITIONS OF PRAVAH.

  • The first round will be online , the groups have to sign in on songdew.com and upload their music and send us the song link in the registration form.
  • Marks will be deducted for exceeding the time limit.

  • Equipment provided will be : • Adequate microphones • Drum set (you can bring along with your own cymbals and chokes/high hats)

  • Bands will have to bring their own musical instruments and special effects.

  • PRAVAH will not be held responsible for the failure of any instrument.

  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.

  • PRAVAH reserves the right to make any last minute change in the rules

Fee

There would be a Rs 500 registration fee for the selected bands.
