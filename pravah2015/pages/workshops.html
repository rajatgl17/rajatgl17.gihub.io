Touch Screen


Overview

This workshop mainly focuses on the students eager to learn working of Touch screen . This workshop helps participants to understand the designing concepts and use of microcontrollers, relays, power supply, microcontroller programming in Embedded 'C'language and also enables them make their own touch screen module.

The duration of this workshop will be two consecutive days, with three hours session and two sessions each day in a total of 6 hours, properly divided into theory and hand on practical sessions. At the end of this workshop, a competition will be organized among the participating students where each participating student will get Certificate of Participation and the Winners will get Certificate of Merit.







Workshop Level :

Intermediate Level

Best Suited for :

All B.Tech./B.E./BCA/B.Sc Students and those who are interested in learning.

KIT :

Every participant of workshop will get a KIT containing all required components.

Schedule

21st - 22nd February 2015. ( 6hrs/day )

Content

Day 1 (Session 1)

  1. Introduction to basics

    • Basics of Electronics
    • Sensors
    • Operational Amplifier
    • Integrating Circuit
    • Interfacing of Sensors
    • Motors and Controlling Circuit
    • Interfacing of relays

  2. Introduction to Microcontrollers

    • This session would deal with the basics of Microcontroller. The focus will be on the AVR series micro controller- ATMega8, which is one of the most powerful and widely used 8 bit micro controller.
    • What is Microcontroller?
    • Difference between Microcontroller and Microprocessor.
    • Microcontroller Architecture and Interfacing.
    • How can we use Microcontroller in our Own Circuits?

Day 1 (Session 2)

  1. Introduction to Programming Languages

    • Programming Languages- Assembly vs Embedded C.
    • Microcontroller Programming using 'Embedded C'.

  2. Introduction to Touch Screen

    • What is Touch Screen
    • Working of Touch Screen
    • Different types of touch screen
    • Merits and Demerits of using Touch Screen
    • Application of touch screen in daily life

  3. Installation of Software and Debugging

    • Writing your First 'Embedded C' Program in AVR Studio.
    • Program Compilation and Debugging.
    • Loading Compiled 'C' Program into Micro-controller

Day 2 (Session 3)

  1. Touch screen
  2. Understanding and making your own touch screen
  3. Introduction to touch screen codes
  4. Working with AVR and touch screen

Day 2 (Session 4)

  1. Development of touch screen control lighting system
  2. Interfacing the touch screen with the micro-controller
  3. Algorithm development for controlling through micro-controller
  4. Controlling and debugging the errors

Ethical hacking


Overview

This workshop mainly focuses on the students eager to be a White Hat Hacker. It features a brief introduction to the world of hacking starting with the importance of ethical hackers and their need in today's world scenario, including to the basics of networking that will help you have a deep understanding of the working of internet.

Later, windows hacking using various methods will be introduced and in the module of steganography you will learn how to hide files in other files in order to protect sensitive data. After the end of the aforementioned module you will be learning phishing in which you will be taught How to hack Facebook Accounts and Gmail Accounts and thus how to avoid it.
