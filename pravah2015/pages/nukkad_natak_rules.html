General Rules
  1. The team size represents the number of people registered as a team. Only these shall be allowed to perform the street play.
  2. Minimum of 8 actors to be included in the team.
  3. Music accompanists are included in the team number stated above. Maximum of 4 can be included in the team.
  4. Teams are expected to perform at the open air venue, on a circular stage with an audience on all sides.
  5. The team size represents the number of people registered as a team. Only these shall be allowed to perform the street play.
  6. Any kind of fluid, live animals, flame, heavy object or any material which has possibility of damaging the stage is not allowed.
  7. Teams will be heavily penalised if they exceed the time limit.
  8. Plays can be based on any theme but since eliminations are being held at public venues, any content directed against a certain political group or religious community is strictly prohibited.
  9. Obscenity (at the discretion of judges) of any kind is not allowed and may lead to disqualification.
  10. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
