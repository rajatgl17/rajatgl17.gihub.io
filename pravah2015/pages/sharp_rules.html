1. The contest will consist of two rounds, Round 1 is on-line and Round 2 is on-site.
  2. Round 1 will be an on-line MCQ contest which will be hosted on sharp.dreambit.in
  3. Round 2 will be 3 hour marathon coding competition hosted on www.hackerearth.com
  4. This is an individual event. No teams are allowed.
  5. For on-site round, systems will be made available with TurboC IDE installed on it. Candidates are allowed to bring their own systems but internet access would be available on allotted systems only.
  6. There is no registration fee for Round - 1 of SHARP, but individuals qualifying for Round - 2 would be charged a registration fee of Rs. 50 only.
  7. All decisions related to contest pertains to Pravah Organizing Committee and its decision would be final.
