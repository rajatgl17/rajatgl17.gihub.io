RULES
  • An entrant may enter the competitions in only one instrument.
  • Audio or video recording and photography of the competitions and winners concerts are strongly discouraged.
  • Participants are not allowed to make any digital modification.
  • Electric guitar players are allowed to use amps only
  • Drum kit can be provided by organizing team on stage max. time limit is 10 mins.(amps on to amps off)
  • Participants are not allowed to use laptops or mixers or any other digital modifiers.
  • All participants must carry their college identity cards.
  • Participants without identity cards will not be allowed.
  • Decisions of judges and organizing team are final.
  • Backing track is allowed.
  • No use of notation stand.
