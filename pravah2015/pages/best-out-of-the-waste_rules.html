General Rules:
1.Participants in a team of atmost four are required to make an outfit with materials provided by the organisers (limited materials).
2.Materials would be given by organisers prior to event and any other material is not allowed.
3.Time limit would be 4 hours.
4.Decision of judges shall be final and binding.
5.One of the team members shall wear the dress after completion.

Judging Criteria:
Overall appeal of the dress, creativity, style, color composition, intelligent use of materials and finishing
