RULES

  1. It is an inter college competition.
  2. All participants must bring their identity cards. Entry without identity card is not allowed.
  3. Competition consists of LAN gaming contest and competition will be held in PAIR.So participants should register themselves in pair.
    IN CASE PARTICIPANTS REGISTER THEMSELVES INDIVIDUALLY THEN PAIR OF PARTICIPANTS WILL BE MADE BY COORDINATORS ACCORDING TO REGISTRATIONS.
  4. Winners will be awarded with goodies.
  5. Registration amount will be Rs.20 individually and Rs.30 in pair.
  6. Competition related stationary will be given at the venue.
