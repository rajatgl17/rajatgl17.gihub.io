Team size: 8-20(including CAs and music accompanists)

Eliminations:

Teams must perform a part of the street play that they will perform in the finals.
All participating teams must mail a short synopsis (50-100 words) of their scripts to any of the
contacts below, at least a week prior to the respective elimination dates.

Time limit - 9-11 mins (empty stage to empty stage)

Finals:

Teams must perform the extended version of the same street play which they have performed in eliminations.

Time limit: 15-20 mins (empty stage to empty stage)

Judging Criteria:
Acting, Voice (sync, modulation, diction), screenplay, script, crowd interaction and overall impact.
