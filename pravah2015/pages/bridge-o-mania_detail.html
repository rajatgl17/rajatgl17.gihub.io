Creativity involves breaking out of established patterns in order to look things in a different way. Here we provide you chance to show your creativity. You have to create a bridge structure using ice-cream sticks which is stable and can bear load. The structure which will bear more load and is stable will be awarded winner. Adhesive material will be provided. The event has duration of two days. First day you have to create a structure in 2 hrs and is kept to for 24 hrs and on next day load will be applied to structure for checking load bearing capacity and stability of structure.
