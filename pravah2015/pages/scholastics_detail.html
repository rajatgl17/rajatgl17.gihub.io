*SKIT, Jaipur*  in association with robotics club proudly present *SCHOLASTIC*:  A National Robotics Championship for Schools.

We are organizing *Scholastic* in a multi-tier format i.e. 3 stages where the first 2 stages will be at schools and the 3rd & the *Grand Final round* will be organized at SKIT, Jaipur during PRAVAH-15 dated from 26th to 28th Feb, 2015.

*1st Stage:*

Our technical team would be visiting schools and demonstrate students to learn about robotics in a very interactive manner. Hands on session on Advanced Line follower Robotics Technology will be provided by the research engineers of robotics club. Each participant would be provided with a "*Certificate of Participation*" in association with * SKIT, Jaipur*

Students from those Schools which are not covered for workshops willing to participate in the competition can also apply, workshop will be conducted from date  16th  to 20th feb 2015. Selection of date will depend on slot provided on the basic on “First come first serve process”.

Workshop fees is included in registration charges during the workshop “ HIGH QUALITY ROBOTICS KIT” (Costs Rs:2700/-) will be supplied by Robotssss to a group of 4 participants of free of cost.The duration of workshop will be of 2 days(6-7 hours/day).

 Workshop objectives:

  • To provide deep inside into robotic building blocks.

  • To impart microcontroller programming skills.

  • To develop robot programing ,assembly and testing skills.

  • To make robot building easy and fun exercise .

  • All theory will be followed by hands on sessions .

  • Providing best knowledge for starting up engineering with a lead at very low cost !


*2nd Stage:*

Just after the completion of the workshop a zonal competition would be organized among the students who have participated in the workshop. The students would be competing with the robotic kit provided to them during the workshop. All the winners i.e. the first 3 teams will be receiving "*Certificate of Merit*" & “*Advanced robotics kit worth INR 5000/-*”.
*3rd Stage:*

The 3rd & the final round would be organized at SKIT,Jaipur during PRAVAH-15  where the winners of the competition i.e. the 2nd round would be invited to come and participate in the final round. They would be representing at SKIT,Jaipur  with other winners from all the other zonal centers.

Top 3 winner teams of Final Round will be awarded with Cash Prize 50,000/-.

Registration fees: 3200/-(For a team of 4 students).
