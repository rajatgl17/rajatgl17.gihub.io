Most Wanted, like other Need for Speed games, is essentially a driving and racing game, where the player selects one car and races against a time limit or other racers to reach a destination.

THE TOURNAMENT:
  • The tournament shall have 4 levels:Prelims, Level II, Level III & Finale.
  • Prelims will be a single player round.
  • Levels II& III will be multiplayer rounds with a maximum number of four players racing against each other.
  • The finale will be played among the top three finalists over LAN
