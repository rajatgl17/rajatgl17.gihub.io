Rules
  • This is a team event.
  • Each team should have 2-3 members.
  • All participants must bring their college identity cards.
  • Participants without identity cards will not be allowed to participate.
  • One member of the team will act the topic given to him/her while the other team members guess the topic.
  • The event consists of 3 rounds:
    1. In the first round the participants have to guess the names of movies.
    2. In second round they have to guess the idioms while one of the team member will draw the clues (not by acting them).
    3. In the third round they guess the names of different products and their taglines.
  • No lip movements are allowed.
  • The time limit for guessing the name or idiom is 1.5 minute in every round.
  • The actor cannot point out objects in the near vicinity.
  • It can lead to deduction of marks or even disqualification.
  • A single word can be broken into any number of parts.
  • Usually any gesture is allowed other than spelling out the words.
  • The decision of the judges will be FINAL & BINDING on all participants.
