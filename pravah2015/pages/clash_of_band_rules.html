Rules
  1. The first round is the online prelims round in which the bands need to register and give us a you tube link of their song and mail it to snkshukla@gmail.com with their complete contact details. On the basis of their submissions a shortlist would be released.
  2. The maximum length of the submission should not exceed 6 minutes.The band should contain a maximum of 8 members.
  3. After the first round, a shortlist of bands would be released who will qualify to perform at the main event at PRAVAH.
  4. The main event at PRAVAH will be the final round.
  5. In the final round at PRAVAH, a group will be required to perform at least two numbers of their choice and one should be their original composition.
  6. The final round performance should be of maximum 25 minutes. (Amps on to amps off inclusive of sound check)
  7. BANDS WILL BE DISQUALIFIED ON THE SPOT FOR MISCONDUCT, OBSCENITY OR FOUL LANGUAGE, AND WILL BE BANNED FROM PERFORMING AT ALL SUBSEQUENT EDITIONS OF PRAVAH.
  8. Marks will be deducted for exceeding the time limit.
  9. Equipment provided will be:Adequate microphones , Drum set (you can bring along with your own cymbals and chokes/high hats)Bands will have to bring their own musical instruments and special effects.
  10. However, programmed music is not allowed.PRAVAH will not be held responsible for the failure of any instrument.
  11. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.The organizing committee reserves the right to make any last minute change in the rules.
