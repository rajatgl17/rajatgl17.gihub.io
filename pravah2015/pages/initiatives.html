Initiatives



Social campaigns act as a medium of providing the people a panorama of the social issues. The indian population is not aware of a tone of things occurring in the society and there is a need for them to have knowledge about their own surroundings. They should be acquainted to such serious issues. Here Pravah takes the initiative to spread awareness amongst the youth, the flag bearing leaders. We discuss many issues and then focus on one area, which we think can be addressed through our festival and where we can make a difference, The organizers will not restrict themselves to the student community, they will also help a couple of NGOs. The students will also be taking forward their campaign.




Bal Mitra

A forum to help and empower the unprivileged children to become strong pillars for the upliftment of the society. Our volunteers try to inculate the values of education, character and cleanness in a friendly manner. For this various activities such as (Nukkad Natak), slogan competitions, poster competition, debates etc. are organized from time to time. The key concept of Bal Mitra is 'All kids need is a little help, little help and someone who believes in them.'

Women Empowerment

Sponsors

It attempts to empower women through various awareness programmes, lectures and events, that apprise them of their social responsibilities and human, legal rights on one hand and mobilize the society to meet the demands of modernizations and globalization. On the other hand various workshops, lectures and activities are organized that aim at developing women as an icon of confidence, intelligence and smartness i.e. Beauty with brains.

PRAVAH would raise a platform for the HeforShe Campaign in the form of street and stage plays, group discussions, poetry recitation, elocution competition etc. These activities would lend a voice to girls who lack access to primary education and health care. Women in all parts of the world suffer violence and discrimination.
