Rules
  • It is a stage play competition
  • Cross college team will be allowed.
  • All participants must bring their college identity cards.
  • Participants without identity cards will not be allowed to participate.
  • Each team should have a minimum of 7 members and max. 15.
  • There will be one person in charge of sound and maximum 3 in charge of light. participants must have to carry laptops with sounds.
  • The time limit must be 10-25 minutes inclusive of performance and setup.
  • The language of the play must be either English or Hindi.
  • No recorded vocals will be allowed.
  • Use of fire/fluid/smoke machines during the act is not permitted.
  • Any vulgarity or disturbing content of any order will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Decision of the judges will be FINAL and BINDING on all teams.
