Anti Chess is a two player Strategy board Games played on a chessboard. Each player begins with 16 pieces. One king, one queen, two rooks, two knights, two bishops and eight pawns. Each of the six piece types moves differently. The OBJECTIVE is to WIN by either 'CHECK MATE' the opponent's king by placing it under an inescapable threat of capture, or keep earning POINTS by losing your piece. Each piece has different points.
