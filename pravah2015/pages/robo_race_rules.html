ROBO RACEGAME



TRACK

  1. Track surface and course may have unevenness.
  2. There might be abrupt angles.
  3. There will be certain obstacle in the race track which will slow down the speed of robot.
  4. Damaging the track or obstacles will lead to deduction of 10 seconds from actual completion of time.
  5. Arena will consist of speed breakers, slippery path, sand pits, etc.
  6. Actual track will be revealed on the day of event.


RULES & REGULATIONS

  1. The team should not consist of more than 4 members.
  2. Each member from same college is not mandatory.
  3. The robot should follow the specification provided. Any deviation from the mentioned specification will lead to disqualification.
  4. Once the race begins, hand touch to robot is not allowed, if do so, then robot would take place to starting position again.
  5. No test practice will be allowed on main arena.
  6. Terminals for charging the battery will not be provided by us.
  7. The decision of judges will be final and abiding. Arguments with judges in any form will lead to disqualification of the team.


BOT SPECIFICATIONS

  1. Lego kits are strictly not allowed in any form.
  2. The dimension of bot should not exceed ( 30*30*30).
  3. The net weight should not exceed 4kgs.
  4. However tolerance of 3% is acceptable.
  5. Wired or wireless robots are allowed.
  6. The length of wire should be long enough to cover atleast 5m of track and wire should remain slack during the complete run.
  7. The maximum allowed voltage to power the bot is 24 V.


WINNING CRITERIA

Time is the only criteria and winners will be declared according to given formula –:
Net time = actual time – deducted time * Deducted time will be taken if destroying the obstacles or bot.

NOTE :- All regular students with a valid identity card of their respective educational institutes are eligible to participate in the event.
