Rules
1. Participants are needed to make a collage based on a theme given on the spot.
2. All required material will be provided.
3. Participants are not allowed to bring any references of any sorts.
4. Time limit of 3 hrs will be provided. Entries after three hours of competition will not be accepted.
5. Only one sheet would be given so participants should take special care so as not to spoil the sheet.
6. The decisions of the judge shall be considered as final and binding.
7. Any explicit content shall lead to straight disqualification.

Judging Criterion
Adherence to theme, Explanation, Attractiveness, Creativity & Tidiness.
