Rules
1. It is an inter college competition
2. All participants should bring their identity cards. Entry without identity card is not allowed.
3. Upper time limit is for the act is 4 (3+1) min . Time limit should be strictly followed.
4. Act may/may not be theme based.
5. Use of props is allowed. The participants must inform for the same in advance.
6. Participants must be present to provide information about lighting and sound.
7. The lyrics of the songs should be decent. Any kind of objectionable lyrics should be strictly avoided.
8. Pre-recorded music should be brought in pen drive in .wav or .mp3 format only.
9. Any kind of fluid, fume or powder is not allowed at stage.
10. Obscenity of any kind is not allowed.
11. Participants will be judged on the basis of technique, difficulty level, facial expressions and costume.
12. The decision of judges is final and binding
13. the participant are required to clear the stage after their performance.
