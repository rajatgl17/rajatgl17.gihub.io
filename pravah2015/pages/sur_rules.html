General Rules
  • Participants are not allowed any accompanists during the elimination rounds.
  • Pure Classical tracks are not allowed, however a classical refrain is allowed.
  • The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
  • No participant would be allowed to carry his/her own instruments.
  • Participant's own karoke would be allowed.


ELEMINATION Round:-
  • Participants can perform any song of their choise.
  • Participants are not allowed any accompanists during the elemination rounds.
  • Judges reserve the right to stop a participant performance at any time.
  • Time limit:- 1-2 minutes
SEMIFINALS:-
  • It will be piano cum per cussion round, common accompanists will be provided.
  • Singers will be provided with a list of songs on the elemination day,from which they can select the song that they will perform.
  • Participants are allowed to bring their karaoke.
  • No participant would be allowed to carry his/her own instruments, they can perform either on arkestra or on karaoke
  • Time limit:-2-4 minutes(including Set up and clearance time)
FINALS:-
  • The final would be judges based round.
  • 3 ERA'S will be given to each participant and from any era a judge can ask him/her to sing a song.
